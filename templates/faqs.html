Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How do I register myself for an account? ➔ To register, click on the "Sign Up" button at the top of the page. You will need to provide a valid email address and create a password. After registering, you will be redirected your dashboard automatically.

2. How do I update my account information ? ➔ To update your account information, log in and go to the "Account" section from side bar. From there, you can change your email, password, and other credentials.

3. How can I submit a support request ? ➔ You can submit a support request by clicking on the "Contact" button located in the navigation bar. From there, you can fill out the form with details about your issue, and our team will respond within 24 - 48 hours.

4. Can I view my raised queries ? ➔ Yes, you can view your queries by navigating to the 'DESK' icon and clicking the "Queries Raised" button.

5. How can I contact live support ? ➔ If you need immediate help, you can use phone feature, available from 9 AM to 5 PM (PST) on weekdays. For non-urgent issues, you can submit a ticket or post in 'Contact'.

6. How do I post a question in the community ? ➔ Once you're logged in, navigate to the "DESK" section, click "Raise Query". Be sure to include a clear title and detailed description of your issue for better responses.

7. What is the difference between a query post and a support ticket ? ➔ Query posts are public, allowing anyone in the community to view and respond. It's a good option for general questions or issues where crowd-sourced help might be faster.
➔ Support tickets are private and go directly to our support team. Use this option for more complex or sensitive issues.

8. Is there a mobile app for the community ? ➔ Currently, we don’t have a mobile app, but the website is fully responsive and works well on mobile devices. We recommend bookmarking the site on your phone for quick access.

9. What should I do if I experience harassment or inappropriate behavior ? ➔ We take community safety seriously. If you encounter harassment, offensive content, or inappropriate behavior, please report it to our moderators immediately by flagging the post or contacting us through the Support Ticket system in the 'Contact' section.

10. What should I include when asking a technical question ? ➔ When asking a technical question, be as specific as possible. Include details such as:
- The product or the software you're using.
- The exact error message.
- Steps you’ve already tried to solve the issue.
- Any relevant logs.
